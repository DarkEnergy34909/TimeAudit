Welcome to TimeAudit

TimeAudit is a time tracking app that helps you understand how you spend your time so you can take control of your day. It allows you to log your activities, view statistics, and set goals to improve your time management and productivity.

Start by going to the calendar page and logging your first activity!

Note that creating an account is not required, but we recommend that you do so to avoid losing your data when you clear your browser's cache.
